\section{Analysis}\label{analysis}

\subsubsection{Observed Trend 1}\label{observed-trend-1}

Two thirds (2/3), 68\%, of Pyber rides are taken in Urban cities, almost
one third ( 1/3), around 27\%, of Piber rides are taken in Suburban
citis, and only very small percentage, 5\%, of Pyber rides are taken in
Rural cities. \#\#\#Observed Trend 2 Similarly, the number of Pyber
drivers are distributed, 68\% in Urban cities, around 27\% in Suburban
cities, and only 5\% in Rural cities. \#\#\#Observed Trend 3 For the
fares, the numbers are slightly different. 63\% in Urban cities, 31\% in
Suburban cities, and around 7\% in Rural cities. It is happening because
the fares are hihger in Suburban and Rural areas than in Urban areas.
The average prices per ride in Rural and Suburban areas are around \$33,
and in Urban areas the average price per ride is around \$25.
